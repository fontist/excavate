== Format Reference

=== Purpose

Technical reference for supported archive formats.

=== Supported Formats

[cols="1,1,1,2,2"]
|===
| Format | Extension(s) | Extractor | Library | Notes

| 7-Zip
| `.7z`, `.exe`
| SevenZipExtractor
| Omnizip
| BCJ2 filter support

| CAB
| `.cab`, `.exe`
| CabExtractor
| Cabriolet
| Salvage mode

| CPIO
| `.cpio`
| CpioExtractor
| Omnizip
| Old and new formats

| GZIP
| `.gz`, `.tar.gz`
| GzipExtractor
| Ruby stdlib
| Magic byte verification

| MSI
| `.msi`
| OleExtractor
| Omnizip
| OLE compound document

| RPM
| `.rpm`
| RpmExtractor
| Omnizip
| Compressed payload

| TAR
| `.tar`
| TarExtractor
| Omnizip
| POSIX, GNU, PAX

| XAR
| `.pkg`, `.xar`
| XarExtractor
| Omnizip
| macOS packages

| XZ
| `.xz`, `.tar.xz`
| XzExtractor
| Omnizip
| LZMA compression

| ZIP
| `.zip`
| ZipExtractor
| Omnizip
| Standard format
|===

=== Extension Mapping

[cols="1,2"]
|===
| Extension | Extractor

| `.cab`
| CabExtractor

| `.cpio`
| CpioExtractor

| `.exe`
| SevenZipExtractor (falls back to CabExtractor)

| `.gz`, `.tgz`
| GzipExtractor

| `.msi`
| OleExtractor

| `.pkg`, `.xar`
| XarExtractor

| `.rpm`
| RpmExtractor

| `.tar`
| TarExtractor

| `.tar.gz`
| GzipExtractor → TarExtractor

| `.tar.xz`, `.txz`
| XzExtractor → TarExtractor

| `.xz`
| XzExtractor

| `.zip`
| ZipExtractor

| `.7z`
| SevenZipExtractor
|===
